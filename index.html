About

Hi there! I'm Mark - a Bristol-based full stack developer with big ideas and a knack for problem solving.

With a background as a creative producer covering all sorts of music and video projects, I've now turned my hand to the fascinating world of coding. A recent graduate from Mayden Academy in Bath, I'm well versed in a variety of front- and back-end technologies including html, css, javascript, PHP, node.js and React.

Take a look at some of my projects below to see what I can do, and feel free to get in touch if you'd like to work together! Bonus points if you're a fan of good food, squash (the sport), or scuba diving.
